Technology

Our revolutionary facial recognition software operate autonomously protecting the customer’s identity, yet allowing
detection of self-disclosure and blacklisting when appropriate.

Our Data Science applications have enabled great leaps forward, within sports betting scenarios.

In the financial services sector, we maintain a long-term relationship with the European Central Bank, powering a data
application that models every financial instrument in every EU member state – an exceptionally complex task against which
we have always delivered.

In 2016, we launched a data management product that built on this experience, and which can be used in any market sector
that handles large scale or complex data.

Our workflow and data management products both harness the power of data to transform the way organisations work, and
we make them available, together, or separately, as the Finworks Platform.

Our unique ability is to interpret the place of data in the world, and to then create software that creates solutions for those
problems that have been identified.

This is challenging, both intellectually and technically, but it creates a kaleidoscope of possibilities. As our clients
understand, these possibilities are not restricted to any particular sector. Every kind of business, market, event or process can
be analysed as data.

We transform organisations in any sector.
