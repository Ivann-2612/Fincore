Background

Fincore’s roots lie in risk management for investment banking. We’ve come a long way since our start in the late 80’

The company’s directors, Predrag Popovic and Soeren Christensen, met while working in risk management at Nomura International in the late 1980’s. They soon realised that there was a gap in the software available for this type of function. So they founded their own company called Finsoft, which rapidly developed a solid reputation for providing innovative and transformational software for the financial services industry.

Finsoft next applied its risk management expertise to the gaming industry, creating a market-defining software platform; the first real time omni-channel for gaming and sports betting. Expansion followed into other sectors, including government and aerospace, through development of business collaboration, process management and workflow software.

The gaming / betting part of the business was sold in 2007 as ‘Finsoft’, and the other businesses were spun-out into a new company. Then ‘Fincore’ was born. However, Fincore re-entered the gaming market in 2012 to great accolade. Our state-of-the-art gaming development software business is now known as Fingaming.
